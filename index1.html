ABOUT US

Voted number 1 in Burleigh for 3 consecutive years, Red Hot Cod is a proud family-owned business with a passion for providing customers with the freshest premium food! We are ultimate life livers and food lovers hoping to share a bit of our passion with the rest of the world. With a few new things added to the menu this year, we hope to keep improving and satisfying customer needs. Red Hot Cod is home of the $5 fish & Chips lunch special, also notable for our wild caught New Zealand fish, clean oil, crispy beer batter and gluten-free chips (not fries!). With a vast range of handmade sauces, salads and Indonesian street food, we believe our business has something unique to offer when compared to any other fish and chip shop.
